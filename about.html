About me

I am an infinitely curious creator - I make for use and for fun - to learn and share with others. I listen to what others need and create useful and usable designs that align with what they imagined and more. I believe in the power of technology to create communities where all individuals can learn and grow.

I am a creative problem-solver - I rely on information and reflection to solve problems.

I value quality, diversity, integrity and above all else: kindness.

I have spent most of my career listening to others, empathising, researching, and creating digital products to support students in a higher education context. I'm excited to bring these skills to user experience design, research, and writing and to partner with organisations that support people to grow and learn. In a way, I have been applying UX principles my whole career. My passion for research and creating solutions that work inspired me to complete an MSc in Psychology (Research Consultation). I have worked in diverse teams, and the last 18 months of my previous job was in a remote context where I could be relied on to complete my work and innovate.

Currently, I am immersing myself in learning UX design, research, and writing. I have completed the Udacity User Experience Nanodegree, UX Writers Collective UX Writing Fundamentals course. I am a member of the Interaction Design Foundation. I have completed these courses through IDF: Becoming a UX Designer From Scratch, Data-Driven Design: Quantitative Research for UX, and User Research: Methods and Best Practices courses.

My interests include applying psychological principles to UX design and research, design thinking and behaviourial economics. I am also learning front-end web development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript).

Education

Master of Science (MSc) degree in Psychology (Research Consultation) at the University of South Africa

I completed my Master's degree in Psychology. During this time I gained in-depth experience in mixed methods (different qualitative and quantitative methods), as well as designing research studies and writing research proposals. The Master's degree was a culmination of my work and study journey during which I combined psychology with computer science and information systems to develop more effective digital student support interventions.

User Experience Nanodegree at Udacity

I completed the User Experience Nanodegree at Udacity to transition to a career in UX. The programme enabled me to complete a design for a mobile app through user research, developing low-fi and high-fi prototypes, handing over to developers, and monitoring app performance. Finally the programme, supported me to develop my first UX portfolio.

UX Writing Fundamentals at UX Writers Collective

This course introduced me to working with voice and tone, best writing practices for UX writing, writing for UI components, UX writing deliverables and working with teams. The course culminated in a practical project to provide a content style guide and provide input for an app design based on the style guide created.

Interaction Design Foundation

I am a member of the Interaction Design Foundation and have completed the following courses: Become a UX Designer from Scratch; Data-Driven Design: Quantitative Research for UX; and User Research: Methods and Best Practices
